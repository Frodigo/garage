Expert Interview: The Future of Work

This month, we interviewed Dr. Maya Patel, Director of Labor Economics at Cambridge University, about evolving workplace trends:

"We're witnessing the most significant transformation in work structures since the industrial revolution. Hybrid models have stabilized at approximately 60% of knowledge worker positions, while AI augmentation is creating entirely new job categories. Companies that approach these changes as opportunities rather than disruptions are experiencing 34% higher retention rates and 28% better recruitment outcomes."
